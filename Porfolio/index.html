About Me

am originally from a small town outside of Tulsa, Oklahoma and moved to Indianapolis, IN about 15 years ago.
For as long as I can remember I have been into process improvement.
It seems every company I have worked with I saw/found new ways to improve processes and/or efficiency.
Most of the time it was due to implementing software to "paint a prettier picture" of metrics relating to performance for that job.
After doing this a few times I realized that it was actually the software that I enjoyed
if I could make it do produce what I really wanted to show then it would make it easier to implement across the company.
After leaving my last job I realized that software development was where my passion was and is what I really want to do moving forward.
I am excited for my journey in tech and to strive to make companies more efficient, their employees jobs easier,
and increase the bottom line all at the same time.
